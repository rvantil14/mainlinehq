# Mainline Sales Outreach Templates

Customize [TRADE] to match the prospect: plumber, HVAC company, electrician, etc.

---

## Email 1: Cold Intro (Day 1)

**Subject line options:**
1. Quick question about [COMPANY NAME]'s phone coverage
2. [COMPANY NAME] is missing calls right now
3. 27% of your inbound calls never get answered

**Body:**

Hey [FIRST NAME],

Ran across [COMPANY NAME] on Google. Solid reviews, good reputation. But here's the thing: industry data shows 27% of calls to [TRADE] businesses go unanswered. At a $[AVG JOB VALUE] average job, that's $3,000-5,000/month walking to your competitors.

We build AI front offices for [TRADE] companies. Answers every call, books the job, sends the invoice, requests the review. One of our clients went from 14 Google reviews to 47 in 90 days.

Worth 15 minutes to see if it fits?

[YOUR NAME]
Mainline
(805) 801-1380

## Phone Script (30-Second Cold Call Opener)

"Hey [FIRST NAME], this is [YOUR NAME] from Mainline. I'll be quick because I know you're probably heading to a job.

We work with [TRADE] companies, and most of them are missing 25-30% of their inbound calls while they're on job sites. That usually works out to $3,000 to $5,000 a month in lost revenue.

We built a system that answers every call, books the appointment, and sends the invoice automatically.

Would it be worth 15 minutes this week to see if it makes sense for [COMPANY NAME]?"

If they say no or not now:
"Totally get it. Can I send you a quick email with a couple of case studies? That way you have it when the timing's right."
